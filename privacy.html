Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nashi Engineering ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Nashi Platform, including its integration with third-party social media APIs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account registration data (name, email address, organisation)
  • Business data entered into the platform (client records, project data, content drafts)
  • Usage logs (actions taken within the platform, timestamps)
  • OAuth tokens for connected third-party services (TikTok, Twitter/X) to enable content publishing

2. TikTok API Integration

This section specifically describes our use of the TikTok Content Posting API.

2a. What We Access

Our platform uses the following TikTok API scopes:

  • video.publish — to upload and publish approved video content to TikTok on behalf of the authorised account
  • video.upload — to stage video content before publishing
  • user.info.basic — to verify the connected TikTok account identity (open_id and display name only)

2b. What We Do NOT Access

We do not request or access the following TikTok data:

  • Follower lists or following lists
  • Direct messages or inbox content
  • Analytics, engagement metrics, or video statistics
  • Comments on any videos
  • Private account information beyond basic identity verification
  • Any data from TikTok accounts other than the one explicitly authorised by the user

2c. How TikTok Data Is Used

TikTok API data is used solely to:

  • Publish video content that has been explicitly approved by an authorised human reviewer in the Nashi Platform dashboard
  • Confirm that the content was successfully delivered to TikTok
  • Store the resulting TikTok Publish ID in the content draft record for audit purposes

No content is ever published automatically. Every piece of content must pass through a human approval workflow before any API call to TikTok is made.

2d. Data Retention

TikTok OAuth access tokens are stored encrypted in our secrets management system (Vaultwarden, with AES-256 encryption) for as long as the integration is active. Tokens are deleted within 30 days if the integration is disconnected. The TikTok Publish ID stored in content draft records is retained indefinitely as part of the audit trail for published content.

2e. User Rights — TikTok Connection

You may revoke the Nashi Platform's access to your TikTok account at any time by:

  • Visiting TikTok → Profile → Settings → Security → Authorised Apps and removing Nashi Platform
  • Contacting us at privacy@nashi.online to request immediate token deletion on our end

Upon revocation, we will delete your TikTok access token from our systems within 72 hours.

2f. TikTok Privacy Policy

Your use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy, available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/row/privacy-policy/en.

3. Twitter/X API Integration

We use Twitter/X OAuth 1.0a to post approved content on behalf of the authorised account. We access the tweet.write scope only. We do not access direct messages, follower lists, or any data beyond the authenticated account's posting capability. Token management and user rights follow the same principles as described for TikTok above (Section 2d and 2e).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with:

  • Social media APIs (TikTok, Twitter/X) that you explicitly authorise, limited to the scopes described above
  • Infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, CDN) under data processing agreements
  • Law enforcement or regulators where required by applicable law

5. Data Security

We implement TLS encryption in transit, role-based access controls, and encrypted secret storage (AES-256) for all API credentials. Access to API tokens is limited to the automated publishing system and authorised platform administrators.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date. For significant changes, we will notify active users by email.

7. Contact

Privacy enquiries: privacy@nashi.online
